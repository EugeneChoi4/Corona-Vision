The outcome of COVID-19 is in our hands.

Here are some things you can do:
Leave a 6-foot space between yourself and others.

Six feet is like...

  • A refrigerator on its side
  • The height of your tall friend
  • 2-3 strides

Wash your hands frequently.

Soap can break down the protective layer of viruses and wash them away. The process takes 20 seconds.
Twenty seconds is like...

  • Singing "Happy Birthday" twice
  • Singing the chorus to "Lose Yourself" by Eminem twice
  • Singing the chorus to "Take on Me" by A-Ha
  • Singing the chorus to "Stayin' Alive" by Bee Gees
